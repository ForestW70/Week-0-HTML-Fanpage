About SOPHIE:

Sophie Xeon, known by their stage name SOPHIE, was a Scottish muscician and artist known for their unique sound and involvemnet in the innovation of "Bubblegum" pop and other experimental pop genres.

On January 30th, 2021, Sophie tragically fell to her death in Athens, Greece at the age of 34. She is remembered by a large scope of artists, as well as many who felt touched by their music.
